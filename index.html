About Us

We're JL Studios and we're

Hello, we're JL Studios! We started off as a 1 person Lego stopmotion animation youtube channel in September 2019. For 2 years, that was all we did. In 2021, JL met COHEJH, and showed him his channel. COHEJH quite liked the channel and soon began working on a JL Studios website. After a while, JL Studios expanded into JL Coding & JL Studios. The JL Studios part makes Lego stopmotion animations, while the JL Coding branch make videogames, software, website and coding rescources. Please enjoy the website, and subscribe to the channel where it all began...
